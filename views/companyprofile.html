Company Profile

The well established V R S group founded in 1987 by V.RajaSeenivasan, a leather technologist with a vision,is run by a team of professionals, qualified in technology and management.

From its humble beginning, VRS Leathers today has evolved continually over 2 decades to become a leading and well recognised manufacturer and exporters of finished leather from India today.

A state of the art & an environment-friendly manufacturing facility in Chennai with a 150 member strong work force & a technically well equipped team helps us churn out a whopping 6 million sq. ft of finished leather in double quick time matching the various needs of our clientele. We are experts in making finished leather for Shoe upper, Leather goods and Garments

An abundantly stocked raw material helps us meet the stringent needs of the market with relative ease. Quality is paramount to us and we follow TQM throughout our entire production process. The hides are sourced from reputed suppliers and processed with care to meet the European norms and standards.

We are exceptionally well known in the industry for our on-time deliveries and an equally competitive pricing model to serve the best needs of the market.

At VRS leathers, we have always believed in a lasting and long standing relationship with our Customers by understanding their specific requirements and then ensure that their needs are met in the best possible way and above all, at a price that definitely offers value for money.

A long standing relationship with our existing customers across the globe stands testimony to our commitment to quality & excellence in matching the best needs of the customers
