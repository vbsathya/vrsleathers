Environmental Policy

We at VRS Leathers Pvt Ltd, manufactures of Finished leathers from wet blue is committed to prevent pollution & Protect environment through environment friendly processes by complying with all applicable compliance obligations related to environment.

We achieve this through following practices;

  • Optimized use of water, energy, compressed air and other natural resources.
  • Applying Recycle, Reuse, Reduce & Recovery methods wherever applicable.
  • Establishing measurable environmental objectives and providing resources to realize the same.
  • Appropriate training to our workforce on sustainable development.

We are also committed to continually improve our environmental performance through effective Management System.
